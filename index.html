📜 Terms & Info

Turnaround Time:

• Small projects typically take 2–7 days
• Large projects may take up to 3 weeks
• Rush orders available upon request (fee may apply)

Communication:
  • Reply within 48 hours or I may remove you from queue
  • Ghosting after confirmation will result in cancellation
  • You'll get check-ins during the process (preview, edits, etc.)
Revisions:
  • 1–2 small revisions included
  • Major revisions may cost extra
  • No edits after final unless I made a mistake

💰 Payment

  • Full payment due after we confirm details
  • Pay via PayPal, CashApp, or another method if we’ve discussed it in advance.
  • Payment plans available for larger orders
